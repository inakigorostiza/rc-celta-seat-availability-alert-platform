# IE-Nergy Launch Brief
## Product Launch Campaign — 6-Week Timeline

### Product Overview

**IE-Nergy** is a new energy drink positioned for university students and young professionals who need clean, sustained focus — for studying, deep work, and creative output, not for partying or extreme sports. It's a deliberate counterpoint to the loud, hyper-masculine codes of the traditional energy drink category (Red Bull, Monster, Bang).

### Formulation

- **Caffeine**: 150mg natural caffeine (green tea + guarana)
- **L-theanine**: For sustained focus without jitters
- **Vitamins**: B-complex blend
- **Calories**: Zero sugar, only 15 calories
- **Flavors**: Yuzu Citrus, Wild Berry, Matcha Mint
- **Format**: 250ml cans

### Pricing & Distribution

- **Price point**: €2.50 per can
- **Launch markets**: Madrid and Barcelona (Spain)
- **Retail channels**: 
  - University campus shops and convenience stores
  - Specialty grocery and health-focused retailers
  - Co-working spaces and modern cafés
  - Online direct-to-consumer (launch phase)
- **Expansion**: Pan-European rollout by year-end (Paris, Berlin, Amsterdam, London)

### Target Audience

**Primary**: University students and young professionals (18-30) who:
- Study or work 8+ hour days
- Want sustained energy without crash, sugar, or jitters
- Reject the "bro energy drink" culture
- Are health-conscious but not wellness-obsessed
- Spend significant time on TikTok, Instagram, and Spotify
- Value discovery and trying new brands
- Look for proof of quality and authenticity (indie brands, transparent formulation)

**Secondary**: Freelancers, creators, graduate students, consultants in early-career roles

### Key Differentiator

**Calm focus, not aggressive stimulation.** IE-Nergy is designed for the moments when you need to think clearly — not when you want to feel hyped. This is the core insight that separates it from every legacy energy drink.

### Launch Goals

- **Volume**: 100,000 cans sold across Madrid and Barcelona in first 90 days
- **Brand**: Build a foundation that positions IE-Nergy as the thinking person's energy drink, enabling clean pan-European scaling
- **Channels**: Establish presence and virality on TikTok; credibility on Instagram; trial conversion via campus activations
- **Retention**: Convert first-time triers to repeat purchasers through subscription model and loyalty mechanics

### Available Channels for Campaign

1. **TikTok** — Where the audience discovers, debates, and shares. Short-form, peer-recommendation style.
2. **Instagram** — Feed + Reels for sustained messaging and aesthetic. For credibility and lifestyle integration.
3. **Spotify audio ads** — Capture the audience during study/work sessions (high intent moment).
4. **University campus activations** — In-person sampling, tabling at libraries and study spaces, partnerships with student orgs.
5. **Co-working & café partnerships** — Product placement, barista recommendations, community-building.
6. **Email** — Direct-to-consumer, trial-to-repeat, community updates. Low volume, high intent.
7. **Search & affiliate** — For capturing "natural energy drink" and "focus supplement" keywords; partner with wellness influencers.

### What NOT to claim

- **Avoid energy drink clichés**: No "unleash," "fuel your day," "crush your goals," "unleash your potential"
- **Avoid pseudo-wellness claims**: No "detox," "optimize," "biohack," or medical claims (focus benefits must stay to "helps you concentrate")
- **Avoid comparing to competitors on features**: Don't say "no sugar like [competitor]" or "more caffeine than [competitor]"
- **Avoid trying to be cool**: Don't force slang or trends. Don't pretend to be irreverent if the brand voice is calm
- **Avoid safety theater**: Don't over-explain ingredients as if students need reassurance. Trust the audience to do research

### Campaign Timeline

- **Week 1-2**: Research competitor positioning; develop core positioning statement and key messages
- **Week 3-4**: Produce channel-specific creative (TikTok scripts, Instagram content, email sequences)
- **Week 5**: Test and refine; campus activation planning
- **Week 6**: Launch coordinated across channels; monitor early response; adjust based on engagement

### Success Metrics

- TikTok: 5M+ impressions in first 30 days; viral coefficient > 1.5 on 3+ videos
- Instagram: 50K+ followers by day 30; engagement rate > 5%
- Campus: 10K+ trial units distributed; 25%+ repeat purchase rate within 60 days
- Email: 2%+ click rate (high for new brand with no list)
- Overall: 100K unit sales by day 90; strong word-of-mouth in target universities
