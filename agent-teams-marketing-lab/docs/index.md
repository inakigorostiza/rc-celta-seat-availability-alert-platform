# Agent Teams Marketing Lab
## A Hands-On Guide to Building Campaigns with Parallel AI Specialists

Welcome to the Agent Teams Marketing Lab — an educational resource for learning how to structure and run marketing campaigns using Claude's experimental Agent Teams feature in Claude Code.

## What You'll Learn

This lab teaches you how to:
- **Structure a marketing brief** for AI teams (specificity matters)
- **Define distinct specialist roles** — Strategist, Copywriter, Researcher — each with unique perspectives and deliverables
- **Spawn and manage multiple Claude instances** working on the same project simultaneously
- **Watch AI specialists coordinate** — sharing findings, challenging ideas, and building on each other's work
- **Evaluate and critique AI-generated marketing** with a marketer's eye, not a technologist's
- **Apply advanced patterns** — persona panels, pre-mortems, competitive war rooms, and more

## The Core Exercise

You'll spawn three AI teammates to build a **launch campaign for IE-Nergy**, a fictional energy drink positioned for students and young professionals who need calm, sustained focus — not hyped-up aggression.

- **The Strategist** develops positioning and messaging
- **The Copywriter** crafts channel-specific creative (Instagram, TikTok, email, search)
- **The Researcher** analyzes competitors and uncovers positioning gaps

You'll watch them work in parallel, each in their own terminal pane, communicating directly with each other.

## Prerequisites

- Claude Code 2.1.32+ ([install here](https://code.claude.com))
- Node.js 18+ ([install here](https://nodejs.org))
- tmux ([install instructions in manual](manual.html))
- A system terminal (macOS Terminal.app, iTerm2, or Linux — not VS Code's integrated terminal)

## How It Works

1. **Read** the manual (takes 30-45 minutes)
2. **Set up** your tmux session and create a project folder
3. **Spawn** three AI teammates with specific roles
4. **Watch** them work in parallel panes
5. **Critique** the deliverables using the framework
6. **Adapt** the templates for your own products

## What You'll Build

After completing the exercise, you'll have:
- `research.md` — Competitive analysis and positioning gaps
- `strategy.md` — Positioning statement, key messages, channel strategy
- `copy.md` — Launch creative across 4 channels
- `campaign-summary.md` — The lead's synthesis and recommendation

Most importantly, you'll have developed **judgment** — the ability to read marketing work critically and identify what's strong, what's generic, and what needs refinement.

## Key Insight

> AI teams produce confident-sounding work. Your job is to read it like an editor. The most valuable skill you'll build is spotting the generic phrases, the missed audience nuances, and the unsupported claims.
